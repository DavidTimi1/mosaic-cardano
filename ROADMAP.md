# Mosaic Product Roadmap

This document outlines the strategic vision and upcoming milestones for Mosaic. It is a living document maintained by the Product team, serving as a bridge between our community's needs and our engineering efforts.

---

## 📍 Current Status: Gimbalabs Hackathon (Phase 1)
*June - August 2026*

Our immediate focus is delivering the core MVP for the Gimbalabs Piece of Pie Hackathon. 

**Goals achieved so far:**
- ✅ Core web application scaffolding
- ✅ User authentication and Neo4j database integration
- ✅ The Studio (Collaborative text editor)
- ✅ Village creation and discovery
- ✅ Cardano Wallet connection and basic signature flow

**Remaining for Hackathon Submission:**
- 🔄 End-to-end on-chain treasury distribution
- 🔄 Polishing the mobile experience
- 🔄 Beta testing with our first pilot community

---

## 🚀 Phase 2: The Open Village
*Post-Hackathon (Q3 - Q4 2026)*

Once the foundation is solid, our focus shifts to lowering the barrier to entry and expanding how value flows.

**Key Features Planned:**
- **Fiat On-Ramps:** Allowing audience members to subscribe and support communities using credit cards, which are automatically converted to ADA for the treasury.
- **Advanced Reputation Badges:** Expanding the on-chain badge system to recognize specific roles (e.g., "Editor", "Moderator", "Top Contributor").
- **Rich Media Studio:** Expanding the editor to support collaborative audio and image uploads for podcasters and visual artists.
- **Cross-posting Integrations:** Allowing communities to instantly publish their finished pieces to Web2 platforms (Twitter/X, Substack) while retaining the canonical link on Mosaic.

---

## 🌍 Phase 3: True Decentralized Governance
*2027*

Moving from a platform to a protocol. Giving communities complete control over their rules.

**Key Features Planned:**
- **Village Constitutions:** Smart-contract enabled governance where communities vote on their treasury distribution percentages.
- **Dynamic Splitting:** Allowing revenue splits to adjust automatically based on community voting or engagement metrics.
- **Mobile Native App:** Launching iOS and Android apps optimized for low-bandwidth environments in emerging markets.
